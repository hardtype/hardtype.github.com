The Distortion Master is a legendary analog distortion pedal, renowned for its rich, tube-like saturation and dynamic response. From classic rock crunch to searing high-gain leads, this pedal delivers a versatile range of tones that have shaped countless records. Its simple three-knob layout—Volume, Tone, and Gain—provides an intuitive yet powerful way to sculpt your sound. Built with premium components in a rugged enclosure, the Distortion Master is designed for the road and the studio alike.

Controls

  • GAIN: Adjusts the amount of distortion
  • TONE: Shapes the EQ of the effect
  • LEVEL: Controls the overall output volume
